Our Products

Sensory Bottles

Our sensory bottles are crafted to engage children at every stage of their development. For infants and toddlers, they are a source of fascination and helping to enhance sensory awareness, calmness, and visual tracking.
As children grow, the bottles become tools for interactive play, such as "I Spy" games, color mixing, and sensory science experiments. They are also perfect for pretend play, providing endless possibilities for imaginative exploration.
With diverse themes and sensory experiences — float, sound, spy, move, and touch — they keep playtime vibrant and engaging, while supporting growth and exploration.

Water Hyacinth Baskets

Our Water Hyacinth Baskets are ideal for little hands to carry their treasures or showcase our sensory bottles. Made from flexible material, these baskets are perfect for both playful exploration and practical storage. They bring charm and functionality to your child's space.
They are part of our baby shower and new arrival sets, designed to complement our sensory bottles beautifully.

Basket size: 36x20x16cm

Playdough Kits

Our playdough kits spark imagination and inspire creativity. Our permanent and seasonal themed kit, complete with curated toys and props, bring each theme to life, making playtime both delightful and enriching. Designed to align with children's interests, these kits encourage deeper exploration, and self-expression.
Our homemade playdough is a sensory delight—non-toxic, safe for little hands, and available in beautiful colors often with glitter and sequins for extra sparkle. Whether scented or unscented, it invites endless creative possibilities.
As children explore with our playdough and themed props, they naturally share their thoughts and feelings, fostering meaningful connections and supporting their growth.

Playdough Balls

Introducing our Playdough Balls—available in sets of 3 or 6, with customizable colors, glitter, and scents.
Perfect for refreshing your playdough kit, adding a splash of fun to your existing props, or gifting as a special treat, these playdough balls are crafted with the same safe, skin-friendly recipe as our playdough kits.
